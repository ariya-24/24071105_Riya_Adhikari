Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ now.year }}

1. Data We Collect

We collect only the essential information required to process your booking:

  • Name and Email Address (for account creation and booking confirmation).
  • Payment Information (simulated for this educational project).
  • Booking Details (dates, hotel selection, guests).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for:

  • Managing your account and login sessions.
  • Processing room reservations and cancellations.
  • Generating sales reports (anonymized where possible).

3. Data Protection (LESP)

We adhere to the principles of the Data Protection Act 2018 and GDPR:

  • Security: Passwords are hashed using PBKDF2 (Werkzeug). We do not store plain-text passwords.
  • Confidentiality: Admin access is restricted to authorized personnel.
  • Integrity: All database interactions use parameterized queries to prevent injection attacks.

4. Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you logged in. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

5. Contact Us

For any privacy concerns, please contact the admin team.
